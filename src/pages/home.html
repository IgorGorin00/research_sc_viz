Evaluating the Impact of Special Economic Zones

Welcome to my Master's Thesis Presentation

This project is a digital, interactive exploration of my master's thesis in which I've conducted an analysis of the Special Economic Zones (SEZs) in India, Russia, and Brazil. Instead of a traditional static presentation, I've decided to present my research findings through this web-based application, offering an interactive and engaging experience for the viewer.

About the Research

My research utilizes the synthetic control methodology to evaluate and compare the impact of Special Economic Zones in India, Russia, and Brazil. SEZs are areas within countries that have unique economic regulations separate from the rest of the country. Their impacts on economic development, foreign direct investment, and the regional economy can be significant and thus, it's crucial to understand their effects.

About This Application

To make this research more accessible and engaging, I have incorporated a variety of visualizations that are interactive and animated. You can navigate through the research findings for each country, understand the methodologies used, and dive deeper into the datasets employed in my analysis.

You may navigate to the pages dedicated to each country using the links below:

  • Russia
  • Brazil

How to Navigate

The navigation bar at the top of the page allows you to select the country of interest. Upon selecting a country, you'll be presented with a detailed analysis of the impacts of SEZs in that country, complete with interactive graphs and charts.
